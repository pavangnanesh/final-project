Net Recovery

After a customer returns a product, the net recovery rate is the total amount of money a company makes back from reselling it after deducting all transportation, processing, refurbishing, and other related expenses. Oftentimes companies miss the mark on realizing significant bottom-line gains by not funneling their decisions through maximizing net recovery.
